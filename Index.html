Biography

Hello, My Name Is Henok Addisu. I was born in Addis Ababa & raised in Bale Robe City. I’m 20 years old and currently studying at Addis Ababa Tegbareid Polytechnic College, Where I’m pursuing Biomedical Equipment Servicing, along with Web Development & Database Administration.
- Since childhood, I’ve always been passionate about IT, Technology, & hands-on Technical work especially fixing things around the house. I Wish That curiosity has grown into a commitment to build solutions that combine Health and Technology.
- I’m deeply grateful to my family. Their unwavering support, guidance and encouragement has shaped who I am today.
- Looking ahead, My dream is to become both a leading Biomedical Engineer & a skilled Full-Stack Developer.
- I want to specialize in both fields to create Innovative, Problem-Solving Technologies especially for those suffering due to illness or lack of access to modern tools.
- I’m inspired by pioneers like Charles Babbage , the father of the computer and Elon Musk The founder of Tesla & SpaceX.
- I'm also deeply respect Ethiopian figures like Dr. Rodas Taddese and Akilu Lemma for their contributions to science and progress.
